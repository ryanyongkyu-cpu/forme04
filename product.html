Description +

An expression of pure femininity, this floor-length evening gown is crafted from the finest Japanese silk charmeuse, known for its luminous drape and feather-light hand. The bias-cut silhouette skims the body with understated sensuality, while a deep cowl back and delicate spaghetti straps lend an air of timeless glamour.


Designed in our Parisian atelier and handfinished by our master seamstresses, this gown embodies the Maison Lumière philosophy — effortless luxury, impeccable construction.
